# 🚀 Hardware Projects | IPD-CP (Innovation & Product Development Certificate Program)

This repository contains the hardware and embedded systems projects developed as part of the **IPD-CP (Innovation & Product Development Certificate Program) Cohort** under the **Centre for Innovation and Product Development (CIPD)**.

The projects focus on robotics, embedded systems, automation, IoT, and Arduino-based product development while applying practical engineering concepts to solve real-world problems.
